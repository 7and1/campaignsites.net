High-intent CTAs

- Start my free audit
- Unlock the checklist
- Send me the templates
- Book a 15-minute review
- Show me the breakdown

- Claim the benchmark report

Tips
- Pair CTAs with a micro-commitment: "No credit card" or "2-minute setup".
- Use action verbs, not nouns.
- Add specificity ("Get the 5-step plan").
